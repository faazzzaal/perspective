Description

Our technical design service takes your project from concept to reality by producing the detailed drawings and specifications required for construction. Once a design direction is approved, our team develops comprehensive construction documents, including architectural plans, structural details, and mechanical/electrical layouts. This service ensures every technical aspect of the project is planned and coordinated, so contractors have a clear blueprint to build from and nothing is left to guesswork.

Benefits

Our architectural work improves people's lives. We work closely with clients to create functional solutions that creatively respond to the needs of individuals and organizations. From corporate facility innovation to commercial developments, we create beautiful, balanced designs fueled by big ideas.

  • Comprehensive Documentation: Complete sets of construction drawings and specifications that leave no detail unchecked, reducing on-site errors.
  • Coordination of Disciplines: Integration of structural, electrical, plumbing, and HVAC details into the design, preventing clashes and ensuring all systems work together seamlessly.
  • Code & Standards Compliance: Technical drawings prepared to meet building codes, safety regulations, and industry standards, facilitating smooth permit approvals.
  • Cost Control: Well-documented plans minimize change orders and material waste, keeping the construction phase on budget.
  • ● Quality Assurance: A thoroughly detailed design means the finished building will closely match the approved vision, with quality materials and methods clearly defined.

Perfect Planning

In technical design, perfect planning is about foresight and precision. Before construction begins, we methodically plan out every component of the project on paper (and in digital models). This includes specifying exact dimensions, materials, and methods for each part of the build. By doing this planning upfront, we catch potential issues or conflicts in the design phase—long before they can impact the construction schedule. The result is a construction process that runs much more smoothly, because crews can follow a well-organized plan. All stakeholders, from engineers to contractors, understand the project requirements clearly, which helps avoid delays, miscommunications, and unexpected costs once building is underway.

Approach

Our technical design approach emphasizes collaboration and accuracy. We work closely with engineers and specialists from all disciplines (structural, mechanical, electrical, etc.) to produce coordinated drawings. Using state-of-the-art CAD and BIM software, our designers generate detailed floor plans, cross-sections, elevations, and construction details for every aspect of the project. We conduct internal reviews and cross-discipline workshops to ensure architectural elements align with engineering requirements. Every detail — down to fixtures and finishes — is documented and scheduled, and we hold regular check-in meetings with you to verify technical decisions stay true to the project’s intent. By the time the technical design package is complete, you have a built-ready plan that contractors can execute confidently, knowing the design has been thoroughly vetted and coordinated.
